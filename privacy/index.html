Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 25, 2026

Your privacy is fundamental to Pafly. Anonymity is not just a feature — it is at the core of what we build. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have.

1. Our Commitment to Anonymity

Pafly is designed so that no user can ever identify another user. Messages are anonymous by design. No personal information is visible between users. The sender and recipient of any message are never revealed to each other.

2. Information We Collect

Account Data

When you use Pafly, we create an anonymous account using Firebase Authentication. No personal information is required — no email, no password, no real identity.

Messages and Content

We store the messages you write, reactions you send, and replies you compose. Messages are stored to enable delivery to other users. We do not analyze your messages for advertising or profiling purposes.

Device Information

We collect a unique device identifier and push notification token to deliver notifications and ensure proper app functionality. We store basic device information (platform, OS version) for technical support and compatibility.

Usage Data

We track writing activity dates (which days you sent a message) to display your writing calendar and streak statistics. We store aggregate counts of messages sent and received. We do not track how you navigate the app, which messages you read, or time spent on screens.

Notification Preferences

We store your notification preference settings (daily reminders, new messages, reactions, delivery notifications) so we can respect your choices about which notifications to receive.

3. How We Use Your Data

  • Deliver your messages to other users anonymously
  • Moderate content to maintain a safe environment
  • Send push notifications based on your preferences
  • Display your writing statistics and streak calendar
  • Ensure fairness in message distribution
  • Prevent abuse and enforce our Terms of Service

4. Content Moderation

Messages are processed through AI-powered content moderation before delivery. This is done solely to filter harmful content (hate speech, violence, harassment, illegal content). Moderation is automated and no human reads your messages during this process.

We do not use your messages to train AI models, build user profiles, or for any purpose beyond moderation and delivery.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data to third parties. We share data only with the following service providers, strictly for app functionality:

  • Firebase (Google) — authentication and push notifications
  • OpenAI — content moderation (message text only, no user identifiers)
  • Microsoft Azure — cloud infrastructure and background processing

We may disclose data if required by law or to protect the rights, safety, or property of Pafly, our users, or the public.

6. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored on secure servers hosted on Microsoft Azure. We use industry-standard security measures including encryption in transit (TLS) and at rest. Authentication tokens are stored securely on your device using platform-provided secure storage.

While we take reasonable precautions to protect your data, no method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. Data Retention

Messages that cannot be delivered expire after 7 days and are removed from active processing. Delivered messages are retained to allow the recipient to view them. Writing calendar data is retained for the duration of your account.

When you delete your account, all your data is permanently removed from our systems, including your messages, reactions, statistics, and account information. This action is irreversible.

8. Your Rights and Choices

  • Delete your account and all associated data at any time from the app settings
  • Control which notifications you receive through notification preferences
  • All messages are moderated for safety before delivery

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have additional rights under applicable data protection laws (such as GDPR or CCPA), including the right to access, correct, or request deletion of your personal data. Contact us to exercise these rights.

9. Children's Privacy

Pafly is not intended for children under the age of 13 (or the applicable minimum age in your jurisdiction). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we become aware that a child has provided us with personal data, we will take steps to delete that information.

10. Local Storage

The app stores minimal data locally on your device for functionality purposes: your authentication token (in secure storage), device identifier, onboarding status, and notification preferences. This data stays on your device and is cleared when you delete your account.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes through the app. Continued use of Pafly after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

12. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or want to exercise your data rights, please contact us at support@pafly-app.com.
